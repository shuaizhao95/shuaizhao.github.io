Biography

Hi! I'm an assistant researcher in the College of Software and Technology at Zhejiang University. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Zhejiang University under the co-supervision of Prof. Fei Wu and Prof. Yi Yang. I am also so lucky to work with Prof. Jiwei Li and Associated Prof. Kun Kuang. My research interests include natural language processing and deep learning. Currently, I study foundation models, including pre-training, fine-tuning, alignment, and their applications on downstream tasks.

Note: I am always interested in taking self-motivated students. Contact me if you are very interested in deep learning and NLP.
